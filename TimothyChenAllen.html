TimothyChenAllen started programming Perl in 1999. He was living in Barcelona and had recently gotten a job as the Technical Director of a small eCommerce company. He read the CamelBook and saw that there was an active world-wide community of Perl Programmers, including a group known as the PerlMongers. Since no active group existed for Barcelona proper, he started one.

TimothyChenAllen moved on to New Orleans in 2004. His home page is at http://www.timallen.org.

He has a 3 year old son named Daniel and a wife named Sònia.
