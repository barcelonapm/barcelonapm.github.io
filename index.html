Barcelona Perl Mongers

Name

Barcelona.pm — Barcelona Perl Mongers

Synopsis

use Perl;
use Acme::PM::Barcelona;

Description

Barcelona.pm is a group of people interested in Perl (also known as Perl Mongers) that are living in Barcelona. Interestingly enough, there's a lot of people in the group that happen to be living outside Barcelona too, so anyone is welcome aboard. Please, start subscribing the mailing list and introduce yourself.

Meetings

Monthly meetings take place the last Thursday. Next meeting is due on Thu, Jun 27 2013 20:00 (CET).

Events

Barcelona.pm organizes courses for beginners and advanced Perl programmers. You can find the information for those events on Curs Perl. We also have an announcements list if you want to keep up with new events.
